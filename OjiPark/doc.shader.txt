Parameters used to compile the main shader:
PHONG - simple Phong equation
PERFRAGMENT - simple Phong equation per fragment
FRESNEL - Phong equation with Fresnel applied to shinies
UV - texture support
SHADOW - shadow rendering support
WGLOW - experimental: white glow effect
BGLOW - experimental: black contour effect
QUANTIFY8 - experimental: color quanitfication, 8 per channel
QUANTIFY4 - experimental: color quanitfication, 4 per channel
ALPHADISCARD - discard fragments with alpha == 0
HEMISPHERE - simplified hemisphere lightning 
BUMP - normal bump map enabled; disables HEMISPHERE;
